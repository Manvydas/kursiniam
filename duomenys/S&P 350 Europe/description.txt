iShares Europe ETF (the Fund), formerly iShares S&P Europe 350 Index Fund, 
is an exchange-traded fund (ETF). The Fund seeks investment results that 
correspond generally to the price and yield performance of the S&P Europe 350
(the Index). The Index measures the performance of the stocks of companies in 
the countries, which includes Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Finland, France, 
Germany, Greece, Ireland, Italy, Luxembourg, the Netherlands, Norway, 
Portugal, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland and the United Kingdom. The Fund 
generally invests at least 90% of its assets in securities of the Index and 
in depositary receipts representing securities of the Index. The Fund's 
investment advisor is BlackRock Fund Advisors.
